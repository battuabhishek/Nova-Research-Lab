Laboratory Equipment

Our core facility houses some of the most advanced computational and wet-lab infrastructure available, accessible to verified institutional partners.

Supercomputing Cluster

1,024 A100 Tensor Core GPUs linked via NVIDIA Quantum InfiniBand, fully dedicated to protein folding and deep simulation rendering.

Cryo-Electron Microscopy

Titan Krios G4 equipped with Falcon 4 direct electron detectors, allowing atomic-level resolution of complex biological targets.

Automated Liquid Handlers

Robotic, high-throughput wet lab lines capable of executing 20,000 discrete biochemical assays per hour with near-zero error margins.
