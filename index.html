Research Areas

Our laboratory operates at the intersection of computational engineering and biomedical discovery.

Computational Protein Folding

Using advanced neural networks to map 3D molecular structures and accelerate drug discovery pipelines.

Biomedical Imaging Pipelines

Processing petabytes of high-throughput microscopic imagery in real-time to identify weak cellular anomalies.

Large Scale Epidemiology

Simulating global pathogen spread using massive multi-agent statistical mechanics and spatiotemporal tracking.
